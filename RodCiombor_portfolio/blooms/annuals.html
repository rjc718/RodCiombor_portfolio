Annuals

Annuals are plants that typically only live for one year. They add spectacular color to your beds in the spring, summer, and fall, but usually do not survive the winter, although some do reseed and come back the next year. We have many varieties of annuals now including petunias, poppies, geraniums, and beautiful fuchsia as pictured on the left. Deadheading, or removing spent blooms, will encourage your annuals to keep blooming.
